IEEE ComSoc Kerala Chapter

6G & BEYOND

IEEE ComSoc Kerala Chapter is organizing the first edision of 6G & BEYOND on 16th of November 2024. Exploring the transformative potential of 6G technology. It serves as a platform where industry experts, researchers, and innovators come together to discuss cutting-edge advancements in communication technology. The summit highlights 6G's potential applications and how these next-gen technologies could significantly enhance global Communications


Register Now!

Mission

The event is committed to propelling the development of 6G with an emasis on secure, resilient communication networks. By fostering innovation and collaboration, the 6G Summit aims to develop networks that bolster disaster response, ensuring uninterrupted connectivity and rapid response capabilities during emergencies.

Vision

The program envisions a future where 6G serves as the foundation of secure and adaptable communication networks, strengthening disaster management by ensuring reliable connectivity in emergencies. Through advanced technologies, the summit aims to enhance global safety and connectivity, supporting communities and critical infrastructure in any crisis.
